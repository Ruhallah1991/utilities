Wavelength Calculator

Definitions

Wavelength (\( \lambda \)): The distance over which a wave's shape repeats.

Electrical Length (\( \theta \)): The length of a transmission line, measured in electrical radians.

Parameter Dimensions

Speed of Light (\( c \)): Measured in meters per second (m/s).

Frequency (\( f \)): Measured in Hertz (Hz).

Physical Length (\( L \)): Measured in meters (m).
