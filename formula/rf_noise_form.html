CMOS and RF Noise Metrics

Noise Figure (NF)

Quantifies how much a device degrades the signal-to-noise ratio.

\[ NF = 10 \log_{10}\left(\frac{SNR_{in}}{SNR_{out}}\right) \]

Units: dB

Definitions: \( SNR_{in} \) and \( SNR_{out} \) are the input and output Signal-to-Noise Ratios.

Thermal Noise

Generated by the random motion of electrons in a resistor.

\[ V_{n} = \sqrt{4kTR\Delta f } \]

Units: V/√Hz

Definitions: \( V_{n} \) is the noise voltage, \( k \) is the Boltzmann constant, \( T \) is the temperature in Kelvin, \( \Delta f \) is the bandwidth, \( R \) is the resistance.

Shot Noise

Caused by the random arrival times of electrons at a barrier.

\[ I_{n} = \sqrt{2qI\Delta f} \]

Units: A/√Hz

Definitions: \( I_{n} \) is the noise current, \( q \) is the electron charge, \( I \) is the DC current, \( \Delta f \) is the bandwidth.

Flicker Noise of CMOS

Describes how the 1/f noise can be modeled considering transistor dimensions \(W\) and \(L\), oxide capacitance \(C_{ox}\), and frequency \(f\).

\[ V_{n,flicker} = \sqrt{\frac{k}{{W \times L \times C_{ox} \times f}}} \]

Units: V/√Hz

Definitions: \( V_{n,flicker} \) is the flicker noise voltage, \( k \) is the flicker noise constant, \( W \) and \( L \) are the transistor dimensions, \( C_{ox} \) is the oxide capacitance, \( f \) is the frequency.

Channel Noise of MOSFET

Represents the thermal noise generated by the channel resistance in MOSFETs, taking into account the noise coefficient \( \gamma \).

\[ I_{n,channel} = \sqrt{4kT\gamma g_{m}} \]

Units: A/√Hz

Definitions: \( I_{n,channel} \) is the channel noise current, \( \gamma \) is the noise coefficient, \( k \) is the Boltzmann constant, \( T \) is the temperature in Kelvin, \( g_{m} \) is the transconductance.
