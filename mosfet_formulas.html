MOSFET Current Formulas

NMOS

Subthreshold: \( I_D = I_0 \cdot e^{\frac{V_{GS}}{V_T}} \)

Saturation: \( I_D = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \mu_n \cdot C_{ox} \cdot \frac{W}{L} \cdot (V_{GS} - V_{th})^2 \)

Linear: \( I_D = \mu_n \cdot C_{ox} \cdot \frac{W}{L} \cdot ((V_{GS} - V_{th})V_{DS} - \frac{V_{DS}^2}{2}) \)

PMOS

Subthreshold: \( I_D = I_0 \cdot e^{-\frac{V_{SG}}{V_T}} \)

Saturation: \( I_D = -\frac{1}{2} \cdot \mu_p \cdot C_{ox} \cdot \frac{W}{L} \cdot (V_{SG} - |V_{th}|)^2 \)

Linear: \( I_D = -\mu_p \cdot C_{ox} \cdot \frac{W}{L} \cdot ((V_{SG} - |V_{th}|)V_{SD} - \frac{V_{SD}^2}{2}) \)
