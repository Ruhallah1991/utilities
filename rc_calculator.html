Advanced RC Filter Calculator

This calculator is designed to compute various parameters of an RC (Resistor-Capacitor) filter circuit. It calculates the time constant (\( \tau \)) as \( \tau = R \times C \), the filter corner frequency as \( f = \frac{1}{2\pi \tau} \), and the rise time based on the resistance and capacitance values you input. The rise time is calculated from 10% to 90% of the final value and is given by \( t_{rise} = 2.2 \times \tau \). Additionally, it estimates the amplitude of the output signal at different time intervals (3τ, 4τ, and 5τ).
