Advanced RC Filter Calculator

This calculator is designed to compute various parameters of an RC (Resistor-Capacitor) filter circuit. It calculates the time constant (tau) as 'tau = R x C', the filter corner frequency as 'f = 1/(2 x pi x tau)', and the rise time based on the resistance and capacitance values you input. The rise time is calculated from 10% to 90% of the final value and is given by 't_rise = 2.2 x tau'. Additionally, it estimates the amplitude of the output signal at different time intervals (3tau, 4tau, and 5tau).
