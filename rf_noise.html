CMOS and RF Noise Metrics

Noise Figure (NF)

Quantifies how much a device degrades the signal-to-noise ratio.

\[ NF = 10 \log_{10}\left(\frac{SNR_{in}}{SNR_{out}}\right) \]

Units: dB

Thermal Noise

Generated by the random motion of electrons in a resistor.

\[ V_{n} = \sqrt{4kTBR} \]

Units: V/√Hz

Shot Noise

Caused by the random arrival times of electrons at a barrier.

\[ I_{n} = \sqrt{2qI\Delta f} \]

Units: A/√Hz

Flicker Noise of CMOS in terms of \(W, L, C_{ox}, f\)

Describes how the 1/f noise can be modeled considering transistor dimensions \(W\) and \(L\), oxide capacitance \(C_{ox}\), and frequency \(f\).

\[ I_{n,flicker} = \sqrt{\frac{k}{{W \times L \times C_{ox} \times f}}} \]

Units: A/√Hz

Channel Noise of MOSFET

Represents the thermal noise generated by the channel resistance in MOSFETs, taking into account the noise coefficient \( \gamma \).

\[ I_{n,channel} = \sqrt{4kT\gamma g_{m}} \]

Units: A/√Hz
